Executive Summary

The threat intelligence platform market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing sophistication and frequency of cyber threats. Valued at USD 12.57 billion in 2024, the market is projected to reach USD 40.03 billion by 2032, expanding at a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 15.59% [6].

This growth underscores the critical need for organizations to adopt advanced threat intelligence (ATI) solutions to proactively identify, analyze, and respond to potential threats. Organizations leveraging ATI platforms have reportedly reduced threat detection time by 42% and improved incident mitigation speed by 37% through automation [6].

Key Finding

Cybersixgill excels in deep and dark web intelligence, leveraging specialized AI (Cybersixgill IQ) to provide actionable insights from hidden sources. Recorded Future offers a broader, enterprise-focused solution, utilizing its powerful Intelligence Graph and extensive AI capabilities.

Cybersixgill

Deep and Dark Web Intelligence Powerhouse

Company Overview

Founded in 2014 and headquartered in Israel, Cybersixgill has established itself as a significant player in cyber threat intelligence, particularly renowned for its expertise in monitoring and analyzing threats originating from the deep and dark web [23].

In November 2024, Cybersixgill was acquired by Bitsight, a leader in cyber risk management, for $115 million [11].

Core Capabilities

  • Automated collection from underground sources
  • Threat actor profiling (95+ million profiles)
  • Multilingual NLP and OCR processing
  • Historical data back to the 1990s

Launched in June 2023, Cybersixgill IQ represents a breakthrough in generative AI for threat intelligence. Unlike simple integrations with general-purpose AI models, Cybersixgill IQ utilizes AI across all features, intelligently responding to customer inquiries with tailored insights [12].

Conclusion and Recommendations

Choosing the Right ATI Platform

Selecting between Cybersixgill and Recorded Future depends on your organization's specific intelligence requirements, integration needs, and strategic priorities.

Choose Cybersixgill if:

  • • Deep and dark web intelligence is your primary concern
  • • You need specialized threat actor insights
  • • Cost-effectiveness for niche capabilities is important
  • • Quick deployment and ease of use are priorities
  • • Early warning of underground threats is critical

Choose Recorded Future if:

  • • You require comprehensive, broad-spectrum intelligence
  • • Extensive integration capabilities are needed
  • • Enterprise-scale deployment is necessary
  • • Multiple intelligence modules are beneficial
  • • You can invest in learning complex capabilities

Regardless of the platform chosen, the imperative for organizations is to adopt a strategy of continuous threat intelligence.

The cyber threat landscape evolves daily with new vulnerabilities, attack vectors, and adversary TTPs. Investing in an ATI platform and the processes to leverage it effectively is an investment in the ongoing security and stability of your organization.

Key Terminology

Threat Intelligence

Evidence-based knowledge about existing or emerging threats

IoC (Indicator of Compromise)

Artifacts that indicate potential intrusion (IPs, domains, hashes)

TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, Procedures)

Methods and patterns of activity used by threat actors

APT (Advanced Persistent Threat)

Sophisticated, prolonged cyberattack campaign
